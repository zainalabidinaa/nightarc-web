Luna Management Portal — What to Build First?

The full portal spans 4 independent subsystems. Pick where to start.

A

Account & Profile Portal

Self-service profile management for all user tiers — avatars, PINs, roles, settings. Replaces raw admin table with a polished consumer experience.

B

Subscription & Billing

Plan tiers, payment flow, invite codes for friends & family. Monetization first — unlocks the premium/F&F distinction everywhere else.

C

Catalog CMS

Editorial control: build collections, upload poster images, attach TMDB/community pack sources. Admin-facing power tool.

D

Addon Marketplace

Visual addon browser — search, install, reorder, configure per profile. No more pasting raw URLs.
