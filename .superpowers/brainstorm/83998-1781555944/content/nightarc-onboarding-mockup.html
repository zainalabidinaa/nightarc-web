Animation Blueprint
Screen 1 → 2
The selected poster scales up (matchedGeometryEffect) to fill the hero slot. Other cards scatter off-screen with staggered .smooth springs. Stars slide up from bottom.
Screen 2 → 3
Hero poster shrinks into the mini-phone corner. Three library rows fly in from right, staggered at 40ms intervals using PhaseAnimator.
Screen 3 → 4
Library fades and compresses upward. Logo mark scales in from 0.3. Login buttons slide up from below.
