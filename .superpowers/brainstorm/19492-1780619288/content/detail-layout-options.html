Which detail page direction feels right for LunaMac?

These are structural mockups for the next pass: episode alignment, neutral styling, better artwork fallback, and a more native macOS feel.

‹ Back

A. Native Centered

Recommended. Keeps the cinematic hero but constrains all content to a readable centered width so episodes never hug the left edge.

B. Poster Forward

Bigger poster and info inside the hero. More cinematic, but it makes long descriptions and episode rows feel lower priority.

C. Compact Inspector

Dense and efficient. Best for power users, but less like a polished streaming app and less consistent with the current Home hero.
