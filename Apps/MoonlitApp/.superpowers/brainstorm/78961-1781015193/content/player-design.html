Liquid Glass volume slider

Frosted pill in top-right. Visible only when controls are shown. Drag left/right to adjust. Uses iOS 26 UIVisualEffectView / SwiftUI .glassEffect for the blur+refraction look from your reference images.

Skip Intro pill

Appears bottom-right above the scrubber when playback position enters a detected intro window. Tapping jumps to intro end. Auto-skips silently if the setting is enabled. Animates in/out with a spring.

Timeline highlights

Small amber dots on the scrubber at chapter/highlight positions — sourced from PublicMetaDB alongside intro timestamps. Toggled by "Show highlights on timeline" in Video Player settings.

IntroDB source

Queried once per episode on load using IMDB ID + S/E numbers. Cached locally so repeat plays don't hit the network. Falls back gracefully if no data found — no button shown.
