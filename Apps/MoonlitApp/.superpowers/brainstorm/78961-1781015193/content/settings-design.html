Design notes
Roles

4 tiers: Admin · Friends & Family · Premium Full · Premium Self-Manage. Role is stored on the profile and synced from your website. No in-app payment.

Removed

Appearance screen is gone entirely. No accent color picker since you confirmed it's not used.

New sections

Video Player screen gets: Skip Intro toggle + IntroDB, Auto-skip toggle, Autoplay next episode, Highlights on timeline, Media Type Player (auto-detect + override), Cache Mode. Subtitles gets: Presets, Font + size + color + outline, Preview with real player screenshot background.

Premium Self-Manage

Same as F&F but the Addons row IS visible and functional — they manage their own addon list instead of inheriting admin's.

Does this settings structure look right? Any rows to add, remove, or rename before we move on to the player design?
