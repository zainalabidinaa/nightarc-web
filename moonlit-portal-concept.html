Common questions
What's the difference between Premium and Premium+?
Premium is pre-configured — content is ready the moment you sign in, no setup needed. Premium+ gives you full control to install and manage your own addons and content sources.
How do invite codes work?
If a Family plan subscriber invites you, you'll receive a code granting Friends and Family access — which means you get access to their configured content without paying separately.
Can I switch plans later?
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade any time from the Subscription page. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Is there a free trial?
New accounts get a 7-day free trial on any paid plan. No credit card required to start.
